CPF Nomination Not Updated
›

Your CPF money is completely separate from your will. It can only be paid to people you have specifically nominated. Without a valid nomination, CPF distributes your funds under Singapore's Intestate Succession Act — which may not match your wishes and takes significantly longer.

Your nomination becomes invalid after marriage or divorce. Many people never update it. Updating takes 10 minutes at cpf.gov.sg — and it's free.

An LPA appoints a trusted person to act on your behalf if you lose mental capacity — due to stroke, dementia, or accident. Without one, your family must apply to court — costing thousands of dollars and taking months, while dealing with your medical situation simultaneously.

Register at opg.gov.sg. The basic LPA Form 1 costs just S$75. It is one of the most important documents you will ever sign.

Many Singaporeans assume their flat "goes to the family" — but this depends entirely on your ownership structure. Check your title deed and ensure your will explicitly addresses your property.

Why This Matters
Honest answers to what most Singaporeans have never been told.
⛩️
Why do I need a columbarium niche?
›
In Singapore, over 80% of the deceased are cremated. A niche is your permanent resting place. Without one pre-arranged, your family must find one urgently under grief — at higher prices with fewer choices.
Securing a niche today means your family makes zero decisions about your resting place when you pass. That is a profound gift.
⚰️
What does a funeral package include?
›
Wake venue, tentage, chairs, hearse, embalming, cremation, ceremony officiant, flowers, joss paper, meals — all decided in advance, all paid for at today's price. Packages are tailored to your religion (Buddhist, Islamic, Christian, Hindu).
Without a package, families overspend out of guilt and make rushed decisions they later regret — in the hardest 48 hours of their lives.
📋
What happens to my CPF when I die?
›
Your CPF is not covered by your will. It can only be paid to nominated beneficiaries. Without a valid nomination, CPF distributes under intestate law — which may not match your wishes. Your nomination also becomes invalid after marriage or divorce.
Updating your CPF nomination takes 10 minutes at cpf.gov.sg. It's free. Don't leave this undone.
⚖️
What is an LPA and do I need one?
›
A Lasting Power of Attorney appoints someone to manage your finances and make welfare decisions if you lose mental capacity. Without one, your family needs a costly court order — taking months, costing thousands.
An LPA costs S$75 to register at opg.gov.sg. It's one of the most important documents you'll ever sign.
🏠
Does my HDB flat automatically go to my family?
›
Under Joint Tenancy: yes, it passes to the surviving co-owner automatically — your will has no effect. Under Tenancy-in-Common: your share goes through your will or intestate law. Many people don't know which type they have.
Check your title deed on the HDB portal — it takes 5 minutes. Then make sure your will addresses it explicitly.
📅
What is Qingming and why pre-arrange it?
›
Qingming (清明) is the annual Chinese tomb-sweeping festival — a deeply important cultural and spiritual tradition for Chinese-Buddhist and Taoist families. A pre-arranged package at Nirvana handles all offerings, joss paper, flowers, and ceremony each year. Your family simply shows up.
This is about honouring your ancestors the right way — without burdening your children with logistics.
📜
I have a will — am I fully covered?
›
A will is essential but it doesn't cover your CPF (needs a separate nomination), your life insurance (beneficiary designation controls this), or your HDB under joint tenancy. And even with a will, if you haven't arranged a niche and funeral package, your family still faces enormous burden.
A will is the foundation — not the whole house. The other gaps still need to be closed.
